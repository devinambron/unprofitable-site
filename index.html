Our Mission

At UNPROFITABLE, we have reimagined what it means to be a holding company. While others chase fleeting profits, we have committed ourselves to the artful science of calculated financial erosion.

Our mandate is clear: achieve consistent, year-over-year negative returns through strategic acquisitions and investments that systematically destroy capital with maximum efficiency.

We measure our success by the depth of our losses, celebrating each quarter's achievement in negative growth with unrelenting dedication to our vision.

The companies in our portfolio understand that in a world obsessed with accumulation, true vision lies in strategic decline.
